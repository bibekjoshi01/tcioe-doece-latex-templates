\subsection{Background}

Air pollution is often associated with outdoor environments, but indoor air can be significantly 
more polluted due to poor ventilation, chemicals, and particulate matter. With rising awareness 
of health issues, real-time monitoring systems that can detect harmful pollutants and automate 
corrective actions are essential. Existing commercial solutions are either expensive or lack 
integration with local control systems.
