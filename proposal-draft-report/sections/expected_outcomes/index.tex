\section{EXPECTED OUTCOMES}

The proposed Smart Indoor Air Quality Monitoring and Control System is expected to achieve the following outcomes:

\begin{itemize}
    \item A fully functional indoor air quality monitoring system capable of measuring CO$_2$, PM2.5, VOC, temperature, and humidity in real-time.
    \item Automated alerts and control mechanisms for fans, purifiers, or ventilation devices when thresholds are exceeded.
    \item A user-friendly IoT dashboard accessible via mobile or web to visualize live and historical IAQ data.
    \item Modular, low-cost design allowing easy scaling to additional sensors or different indoor environments.
    \item Increased awareness of indoor air quality and its effects on health, enabling proactive environmental management.
\end{itemize}
