\section*{ABSTRACT}  
\phantomsection
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{ABSTRACT}

Indoor air quality (IAQ) significantly impacts human health, productivity, and comfort. 
This project proposes a low-cost, real-time monitoring and control system for indoor 
nvironments using air quality sensors (CO₂, PM2.5, VOC), temperature and humidity sensors, 
and IoT connectivity. The system will collect data, analyze environmental conditions, 
and trigger alerts or control devices (e.g., ventilation or purifiers) to maintain healthy 
indoor air. This project aims to enhance awareness of indoor air quality and enable 
automated environmental control in homes, offices, and classrooms.

\vspace{0.5cm}

\textit{Keywords: Directional Antenna Alignment, Embedded Systems, Policy-based Control, Reinforcement Learning, RSSI-based Optimization, Wireless Communication}
